  • The Inspection

    First we inspect for any damage from your last guests. We notify you of any maintaince or repairs that are needed.

    Second we preform a detailed cleaning to make sure the home is sparkaling clean for your next guests. We restock supplies for the next guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in my pricing?

The base price ($129/$159/$199/+) includes
- The Inspection
- The Cleaning
- Restocking Supplies
- A Welcome Letter

The gift (locally roasted coffee beans) are avaliable at an additional cost.

Are there any long-term contracts?

No
There are no long-term contracts. You can easily sign up and cancal at anytime.

Do you use your own cleaning supplies?

Yes
We bring all our own cleaning supplies.

Will you restock supplies for guests?

Yes
We have a standard list of what we will restock. These items will be fill to last guest at least 7 days. These supplies are included in the base price.
- Dish soap
- Dish sponge
- Hand soap (kitchen and bathrooms)
- Paper towels
- Multi-purpose cleaning spray
- Shampoo / Conditioner / Body Wash
- Toilet Paper
- Laundry Detergent
Owner must supply any items they want that are not on our list.

What is the "Welcome Letter & Gift"?

The Letter
At the end of each clean we leave a standard letter for the guests, from you (the host). This gives a personal touch and ensures a better review.
The Gift
For an additional cost we can leave a small gift for your guests.
Our standard gift is locally roasted coffee beans.


What if I am not satified with my cleaning?

We guarantee each clean
We guarantee 4+ star cleaning reviews on every clean or your money back (conditions apply).
If upon arrival a guest complains about the cleanliness of the rental we will come to the rental to inspect the complaint. If the unit is not cleaned properly we will refund the host for the cleaning and finish the cleaning to the guest standards.

How do I schedule and pay for cleanings?

Scheduling
We automatically sync calendars, which allows us to automatically confirm cleanings when guest book. No more email and texting scheduling.

Payment
Payments are automatically made when the cleaning is complete.
